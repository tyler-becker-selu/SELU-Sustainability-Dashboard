Southeastern: Sustainability with a vision.

Welcome to the Southeastern Louisiana University's Sustainability Center. We are committed to creating a fully self-sustaining university, and you can view our efforts on this website. See for yourself how efficient the systems are, learn more about the technology, and find out what you can do to get involved!

Longterm Goals:

  • 80% Self Sufficieny
  • 80% of biofuels produced
  • 80% recycling and 20% landfills
  • 80% of trees produced on campus
  • Provide hands-on learning environment.
  • Educational outreach to elementary, middle school studdents

Current Initiatives:

  • Photovoltaic
  • Photothermal
  • Geothermal

How to get involved:

  • Spread the word.
  • Recycle!
